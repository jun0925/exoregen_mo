스위스 처방전

Pierre E.H. Bottiglieri

EXOREGEN EXPERT

'엑소리젠(EXOREGEN)'만의 명품 포뮬라는 세계적인 명품화장품인 '라프레리(la prairie)','발몽(VALMONT)' 등을 처방한 스위스 '피에르 보띠겔리 박사'의 PAP Cosmetics Sciences SA 의 스페셜 처방전으로 개발되었습니다.

보띠겔리 박사는 스위스의 글로벌 코스메틱 그룹의 Product , Manufacture 35년 경력의 Famous Expert입니다. 그가 현재까지 개발한 포뮬러와 기술은 스위스 특허는 물론 전세계 명품화장품과 코슈메디컬브랜드 개발에 핵심기술이고, 그는 하이앤드 기능성 명품 포뮬러 최고의 권위자입니다.

엑소리젠의 특허원료 엑소좀은 국내에서 그 기술을 인정받아 명품화장품에 수출되는 원료였습니다. 원료하나만으로도 불필요한 중첩이 필요 없었고 기술하나만으로도 피부가 주는 모든 신호를 이해하는 설계가 가능했습니다. 그가 인정한 그 원료에 그의 35년 노하우의 스위스처방전으로 우리에게 헤리티지로 남길 명품화장품으로 탄생했습니다.

He has served in leadership roles with several Swiss internationally orientated companies as Research & Development Chemist, Scientific Director and Advisor, Managing Director and Member of several Executive Boards of Directors as well as Founder & Chairperson.

In the course of his career he undertook and directed programs for conceptualization, development and formulations of skin and body care products for top leading international brands known around the world amongst the most prestigious Swiss signatures. He directed R&D programs for numerous multinational groups actively present in various kind of markets segments such as medical clinics, spas, pharmacies, luxury perfumeries, fashion house, beauty salons, departments stores, retail shops, mail order as well as direct sales and multi-level marketing systems in a worldwide environment in Europe, Americas, Australia and Asia.

He is founder and Chairman of PAP Cosmetic Sciences SA/Co.,Ltd Switzerland (1992) and of PAP Health Management SA/Co.,Ltd Switzerland (2010) entities known as service, advising and counselling companies dedicated to develop solutions in the fields of human being health, wellbeing, wellness, nutrition, skin and body care including customized revitalisation programs and related products

He has been a member of the Swiss Society of Cosmetic Chemists (SwissSCC) since 1972 and served as President from 1990 until 2000; he was for many years IFSCC co-ordinator. He joined the Praesidium of the International Federation of Societies of Cosmetic Chemists (IFSCC) as Treasurer from 1996 until 2001, then as Appointee and as Honorary Auditor. In 1995 he was Chairman of the IFSCC Between-Congress Conference which took place in Montreux Switzerland.

He has been awarded Honorary Member of the SwissSCC. (2006)

He has published numerous papers and has authored as inventor for several Swiss and American patents (active substances complexes, cosmetics formulas, concepts of application, etc.). He has been recognized as speaker in several national and international congresses and conventions.
